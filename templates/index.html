FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve answered the most common ones to help you get started fast and feel confident about using Looma.

Do I need to train it or set anything up?

Nope. Just connect your tools and Looma starts learning from your workflow instantly. No prompts, no configs.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Everything runs on encrypted infrastructure and your data is never sold or shared. You stay in control at all times.

Can I use this with my team?

Yup. The Team plan lets you share context, recall across users, and manage everything from one dashboard.

Will it work with how I already get things done?

Yeah — Looma adapts to your workflow, not the other way around. Whether you write things down, talk them out, or just wing it, it fits right in.

Is there a free version?

Definitely. The Free plan gives you access to the core features so you can try it out without a credit card.

How smart is it, really?

Smart enough to suggest tasks before you even realize they exist. It doesn’t just respond — it thinks ahead.
